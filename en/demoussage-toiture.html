Professional roof moss removal

Why remove moss from your roof?

Moss and lichens are more than just an aesthetic issue. They retain moisture, lift tiles and accelerate the deterioration of your roofing. Regular moss removal extends your roof's lifespan by several years.

Cleaning vs. moss removal: what's the difference?

Cleaning removes surface dirt. Moss removal goes further: it treats moss in depth with a biocide product that prevents regrowth for several years. It is both a curative AND preventive treatment.

Our 3-step method

  • Mechanical cleaning — Removal of moss and debris
  • Treatment application — Certibiocide certified biocide product, environmentally friendly
  • Long-lasting protection — The treatment continues to act for 2 to 4 years

Certibiocide certification — LSR Habitat is certified for the application of biocide products. Guarantee of an effective treatment compliant with regulations.
