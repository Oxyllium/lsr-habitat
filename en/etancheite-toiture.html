Our waterproofing solutions

Why treat your roof's waterproofing?

A porous roof absorbs water like a sponge. The result: micro-cracks, frost damage, leaks, mold in the attic. Water-repellent treatment creates an invisible barrier that makes water bead off the surface without changing the look of your tiles.

Our waterproofing services

  • Water-repellent treatment — Long-lasting waterproofing of your tiles
  • Ridge repairs — Restoration of joints and seals
  • Verge repairs — Protection of roof edges
  • Leak detection and repair — Precise diagnosis, targeted intervention
  • Anti-leak treatment — Solutions adapted to every type of roof covering

Did you know? A professional-grade water-repellent treatment protects your roof for 5 to 10 years. It's an investment that saves you from far more expensive repairs.

Our approach

  • Prior cleaning if needed (included in the quote)
  • High-performance professional water-repellent products
  • Compliance with building codes and current standards
  • Post-intervention follow-up and maintenance advice
